We Have A Concentrated Approach To Portfolio Value-Add

Sales, marketing and customer success issues are the most common impediments to successful go-to-market motions. Since Bowery Capital’s first investment in 2013, our Acceleration Team has focused on post-investment support across three key support functions: Talent, Growth, and Business Development.

Talent

People are the heartbeat of any business. Hiring and retaining great talent is critical to the success of any high-growth startup and we support our founders through the entire employee lifecycle. From day one, we coach our founders in establishing people and talent best practices, and we roll up our sleeves to own the end-to-end hiring process for our portfolio’s first revenue hires.

Our human capital expertise is full-stack: from headcount planning, spec creation, compensation planning, sourcing and interviewing to onboarding, performance management and recognition, development, retention, and offboarding.

Growth

Revenue is the lifeblood of any business. For VC-backed startups, it’s also one of the best early indicators of traction and product-market fit. We help founders build a strong GTM foundation by implementing best practice strategies, processes, and technology across sales, marketing, and customer success. With the right groundwork in place, our founders can focus their time on high-leverage activities.

Our growth expertise includes: ICP & buyer persona mapping, messaging, outbound strategy, and sales automation, sales process and qualification frameworks, pricing strategy, CRM setup and optimization, marketing collateral design, creating demand-gen engine, and managing customer lifecycle.

Business Development

Business development is the sustenance that keeps our pipelines full. Warm introductions to your target ICP can jumpstart your sales process and lock in your first referenceable customers. We are focused on providing our founders with the right prospective customer introductions at the right time.

Drawing on our network of over 5,000 IT buyers, we contribute to our portfolio companies’ qualified pipeline on a weekly basis. We continually grow this network to maximize market access across sectors and maximize opportunities for revenue expansion.
