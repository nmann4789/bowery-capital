Investment Strategy

We believe the shift to next generation software is a massive opportunity at roughly 400 billion dollars driven primarily by the rise of the internet native. Since our founding in 2013, we continue to believe we are in the early days of this shift. At Bowery Capital, our thesis is to capitalize on this shift to the next generation of spend by identifying and investing in the absolute best software businesses leading this charge.

Focused on Emerging B2B Technologies

We believe that the next ten years will be the most transformational period in the history of modern business as the impact of emerging technologies is realized. Our Investment Team aims to be the best possible thought partner to founders seeking to capitalize on these unprecedented trends.

Helping Founders Scale Revenue

Bowery Capital’s Acceleration Team helps founders solve the single biggest challenge they will face in their formative years: building an effective marketing, sales, and customer success engine. Post-investment, we arm our portfolio companies with resources, experts, and services proven to accelerate early-stage growth.

Out Investment Process

1

Intro Phase

Week 1

  • Meeting with 1-2 members of the Bowery Investment Team
  • Discussion in twice-weekly partner meeting (Tues-Thur)
  • Internal diligence + potential follow-up questions to fully understand founder vision
2

Due Diligence Phase

Week 2

  • 2nd meetings with follow-up diligence & product demos
  • Intros to connect remainder of teams on both sides
  • Finalization of internal diligence: research, calls, references
3

Confirmatory Phase

Week 3

  • Discussion/negotiation of terms, structure, syndicate
  • Final meeting: remaining questions on both sides
  • Final partner meeting: decision + move to term sheet/commitment

We Have A Concentrated Approach To Portfolio Value-Add

Sales, marketing and customer success issues are the most common impediments to successful go-to-market motions. Since Bowery Capital’s first investment in 2013, our Acceleration Team has focused on post-investment support across three key support functions: Talent, Growth, and Business Development.

Growth

Revenue is the lifeblood of any business. For VC-backed startups, it’s also one of the best early indicators of traction and product-market fit. We help founders build a strong GTM foundation by implementing best practice strategies, processes, and technology across sales, marketing, and customer success. With the right groundwork in place, our founders can focus their time on high-leverage activities.

Talent
