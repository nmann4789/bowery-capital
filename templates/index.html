Out Investment Process

1

Intro Phase

Week 1

  • Meeting with 1-2 members of the Bowery Investment Team
  • Discussion in twice-weekly partner meeting (Tues-Thur)
  • Internal diligence + potential follow-up questions to fully understand founder vision
2

Due Diligence Phase

Week 2

  • 2nd meetings with follow-up diligence & product demos
  • Intros to connect remainder of teams on both sides
  • Finalization of internal diligence: research, calls, references
3

Confirmatory Phase

Week 3

  • Discussion/negotiation of terms, structure, syndicate
  • Final meeting: remaining questions on both sides
  • Final partner meeting: decision + move to term sheet/commitment

We Have A Concentrated Approach To Portfolio Value-Add

Sales, marketing and customer success challenges are the most common impediments to successful go-to-market motions. Since Bowery Capital’s first investment in 2013, our Acceleration Team has focused on post-investment support across three key support functions: Growth, Talent, and Business Development.

Growth

Revenue is the lifeblood of any business. For VC-backed startups, it’s also one of the best early indicators of traction and product-market fit. We help founders build a strong GTM foundation by implementing best practice strategies, processes, and technology across sales, marketing, and customer success. With the right groundwork in place, our founders can focus their time on high-leverage activities.

Our Blog

Talent

People are the heartbeat of any business. Hiring and retaining great talent is critical to the success of any high-growth startup and we support our founders through the entire employee lifecycle. From day one, we coach our founders in establishing people and talent best practices, and we roll up our sleeves to own the end-to-end hiring process for our portfolio’s first revenue hires.

Business Development

Business development is the sustenance that keeps our pipelines full. Warm introductions to your target ICP can jumpstart your sales process and lock in your first referenceable customers. We are focused on providing our founders with the right prospective customer introductions at the right time. We also help our founders create fruitful referral partnerships.
